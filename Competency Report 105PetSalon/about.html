About Us

The Fashion Pet Salon is a category-defining health and wellness company focused on improving the lives of pets, pet parents and our own Fashion Pet Salon partners. Since our founding in 2015, we've been trailblazing new standards in pet care, delivering comprehensive wellness solutions through our products and services, and creating communities that deepen the pet-pet parent bond. We operate more than 5 locations accross the U.S , Mexico and Puerto Rico. The Pet Fashion Salon Foundation is a non-profit Organization and has invested thousands of dollars since it was created to help promote and improve the welfare of companion animals.
